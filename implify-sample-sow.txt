2. CLIENT BACKGROUND

TechFlow Solutions is a Series C B2B SaaS company headquartered in Austin, Texas with offices in London and Singapore. Founded in 2019, TechFlow provides supply chain visibility software to mid-market manufacturers across North America and Europe.

Current state:
- 340 employees, 87 in sales and sales operations
- $42M ARR, targeting $65M by end of FY2026
- Currently using HubSpot CRM (legacy, 4 years of data)
- Spreadsheet-based forecasting and pipeline management
- No formal CPQ process; quoting done manually in Excel
- Separate instances for NA and EMEA teams with no unified view
- Compliance requirements: SOC 2 Type II, GDPR, Illinois BIPA

Key stakeholders:
- Sarah Chen — VP Sales Operations (Executive Sponsor)
- Marcus Rodriguez — Director of IT (Technical Lead)
- Priya Patel — Head of Revenue Operations (Business Lead)
- James Whitfield — Sales Director EMEA
- Compliance: Legal team review required for all data flows

3. SCOPE OF WORK

3.1 IN SCOPE

Phase 1 — Discovery & Design (Weeks 1-3)
- Current state assessment and process documentation
- Future state design workshops (3 sessions × 4 hours)
- Data audit of HubSpot export (estimated 47,000 contact records, 12,000 accounts, 8,500 opportunities)
- Technical architecture design
- Integration requirements documentation

Phase 2 — Configuration (Weeks 4-8)
- Sales Cloud setup: Lead, Contact, Account, Opportunity objects
- Custom fields: 34 agreed custom fields across objects
- Page layouts and record types (NA and EMEA variants)
- Opportunity stages mapped to TechFlow's 7-stage pipeline
- Product catalog setup (142 SKUs)
- Basic CPQ configuration (standard pricing rules, no complex bundling)
- Territory management: 12 NA territories, 6 EMEA territories
- Role hierarchy and sharing rules
- Reports and dashboards: 22 standard reports, 8 executive dashboards
- Email templates: 15 sales templates

Phase 3 — Data Migration (Weeks 6-9, overlapping)
- HubSpot to Salesforce data mapping
- Data cleansing and deduplication
- Test migration (2 rounds)
- Production migration
- Data validation sign-off

Phase 4 — Integrations (Weeks 7-11)
- Outreach.io (sales engagement) — bi-directional sync
- Gong (call intelligence) — activity logging to Salesforce
- NetSuite (ERP) — closed-won opportunity to order creation
- DocuSign — contract execution from opportunity
- Slack — deal alert notifications

Phase 5 — Training & Go-Live (Weeks 12-16)
- Admin training: 2 days for Marcus Rodriguez + 2 internal admins
- Sales rep training: 4 sessions × 90 minutes (cohorts by region)
- Manager and executive training: 1 session × 2 hours
- Train-the-trainer for Sales Operations
- Hypercare support: 30 days post go-live (response SLA: 4 hours)
- Documentation: Admin guide, user guide, integration runbook

3.2 OUT OF SCOPE
- Salesforce Marketing Cloud (separate engagement)
- Advanced CPQ (product bundles, complex pricing tiers)
- Custom mobile application development
- Data from systems other than HubSpot
- Third-party AppExchange installations beyond listed integrations
- Ongoing managed services post hypercare period

6. DEPENDENCIES AND ASSUMPTIONS

6.1 Client Dependencies
- HubSpot data export must be provided by end of Week 3
- Client to provide 3 named admins with Salesforce system administrator access
- Client stakeholders must be available for workshops (Sarah Chen, Marcus Rodriguez, Priya Patel minimum)
- NetSuite API credentials and sandbox access required by Week 6
- Legal review of data processing addendum must complete by Week 4
- Change freeze on HubSpot: no new records after Week 12

6.2 Assumptions
- Salesforce licenses already purchased (87 Enterprise licenses confirmed)
- Client IT team handles SSO/SAML configuration (Okta)
- GDPR data mapping for EU customer records to be provided by Client legal team
- Outreach.io contract renewed before integration phase begins
- Exchange rates for EMEA reporting locked at implementation start

6.3 Known Risks
- HubSpot data quality: preliminary audit suggests 23% duplicate rate in contacts — deduplication may take longer than estimated
- EMEA data residency: Client legal team has not confirmed EU data storage requirements — may require Frankfurt region org
- NetSuite version: Client running NetSuite 2022.1 — integration approach TBD pending API review
- Key person risk: Marcus Rodriguez sole technical contact; escalation path needed
